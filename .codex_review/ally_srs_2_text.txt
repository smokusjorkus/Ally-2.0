1. ​Introduction 
 
1.1. ​ Purpose  
ALLY is an AI-powered web platform designed to improve access to legal information 
and services for Filipino citizens, particularly those who face challenges in obtaining qualified 
legal representation or understanding their rights under Philippine law. This version of ALLY 
represents an enhanced and refined iteration of a previously developed system, incorporating 
improvements in functionality, system architecture, security, and overall user experience. 
This Software Requirements Specification (SRS) defines the complete set of 
functional, non-functional, and interface requirements for the improved ALLY platform. It 
builds upon the foundation of the earlier version by introducing optimized features, expanded 
capabilities, and updated technical standards. The document serves as the primary reference 
for system design, development, testing, and deployment, ensuring a clear, consistent, and 
shared understanding among all stakeholders, including development teams, quality 
assurance personnel, and project management. 
1.2. ​ Scope 
​
The ALLY platform is a secure, AI-powered, web-based legal support infrastructure 
designed to improve access to preliminary legal information and streamline legal workflow 
management between Filipino citizens and authorized legal practitioners. It serves as a 
technology-driven intermediary that helps users access legal resources, organize legal 
concerns, manage case-related information, and collaborate with qualified legal practitioners 
within a secure digital environment. 
The system architecture and functional design are centered on the following key objectives: 
AI Model Optimization and Domain-Restricted Legal Assistance​
The platform incorporates domain-restricted AI models designed to improve the accuracy, 
clarity, and relevance of preliminary legal information. These models use verified Philippine 
legal datasets, legal references, and contextual retrieval techniques to support legal concern 
classification, case relevance matching, and user comprehension. The AI module applies 
intent classification and contextual guardrails to limit responses to Philippine legal topics and 
reject queries outside the supported legal domain. The system also supports periodic 
evaluation of AI outputs, retrieval accuracy, and out-of-scope response handling to support 
future refinement.

Legal API and Verified Legal Repository Integration​
The platform retrieves legal references through available legal APIs and a curated internal 
legal repository containing verified Philippine laws, jurisprudence, ordinances, and related 
legal materials. Where direct API integration is limited, the internal repository will serve as the 
primary source of verified legal references gathered from official government sources and 
recognized legal publications. 
Legal Proceedings and Case Structuring​
ALLY aligns user-submitted concerns with structured legal workflow stages to support 
organized case handling. These stages may include initial inquiry, legal concern classification, 
document preparation, practitioner review, consultation scheduling, case progress tracking, 
and closure or archival. The system helps users understand the general procedural flow of 
their concerns while ensuring that formal legal decisions remain under the authority of 
qualified legal practitioners. 
Shared Workspaces and Collaboration Features​
The system provides secure, role-based shared workspaces for collaboration between clients, 
legal practitioners, and authorized personnel. These workspaces support controlled document 
access, secure communication, task coordination, case updates, and centralized case 
handling to reduce fragmented communication and improve workflow efficiency. 
Detailed Documentation and Audit Trail Features​
ALLY maintains comprehensive records of significant system activities, including user 
interactions, AI query submissions, retrieved legal sources, document uploads, document 
modifications, case updates, and administrative actions. These audit trails support 
transparency, traceability, accountability, and compliance monitoring for both users and 
system administrators. 
Data Privacy, Security, and Compliance​
ALLY enforces strict data protection measures, including secure authentication, role-based 
access control, encryption, protected data storage, activity monitoring, and secure API 
access. The platform is designed to comply with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 and applicable 
legal ethics standards to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and proper handling of sensitive 
legal and personal information.

Constraints and Limitations 
Functional Constraints​
The platform provides AI-assisted preliminary legal information and structured workflow 
support but does not replace licensed legal professionals, courts, or official legal institutions. 
AI-generated outputs are limited to informational and preparatory use and must be reviewed 
or validated by qualified legal practitioners before being used for formal legal decisions or 
actions. The system supports case organization, documentation, and collaboration but does 
not directly represent users, file cases, or execute formal legal proceedings on their behalf. 
Technical Constraints​
Core platform features require a stable internet connection for full functionality. System 
performance may vary depending on user load, network conditions, hosting resources, and 
external service availability. Integration with legal APIs and third-party services depends on 
the availability, reliability, access policies, and technical limitations of those providers. 
Operational Constraints​
Advanced AI optimization, legal dataset expansion, API integration, and collaboration features 
are dependent on available development resources and phased implementation. Some 
features may be introduced incrementally through future updates, especially those requiring 
external legal data access, advanced model evaluation, or institutional participation. 
Legal and Regulatory Constraints​
The platform must comply with Philippine data privacy laws, legal practice regulations, and 
ethical standards governing legal professionals. All system functionalities must be designed to 
prevent unauthorized practice of law, misuse of legal information, unauthorized access to 
sensitive records, and improper handling of personal or legal data.

2. ​Overall Description 
2.1. ​ Product perspective 
​
ALLY is an AI-enhanced legal assistance and workflow management platform 
designed to improve access to preliminary legal information and streamline legal collaboration 
within the Philippine legal context. The platform serves as a supplementary legal support 
system that assists citizens and legal practitioners by providing accessible, organized, and 
secure digital legal services without replacing formal legal institutions, courts, or professional 
legal representation. 
The system simplifies the process of obtaining preliminary legal assistance by integrating 
artificial intelligence, contextual legal retrieval mechanisms, and verified Philippine legal 
datasets. Through AI-assisted legal inquiry and domain-restricted legal intelligence, users can 
receive relevant legal information based on Philippine laws, jurisprudence, statutory 
references, and related legal materials. To reduce irrelevant or out-of-scope responses, the 
platform applies intent classification and contextual guardrails that restrict AI-generated 
outputs to supported legal topics. 
ALLY integrates multiple technologies and external services to support its functionality. These 
include AI-driven legal assistance, semantic analysis, legal APIs or curated legal repositories 
for legal data retrieval, secure authentication using JSON Web Tokens (JWT), role-based 
access control (RBAC), encrypted communication protocols, cloud-based collaboration tools, 
and secure database systems for legal case and document management. 
The platform follows a secure client-server web architecture and is accessible through 
modern desktop and mobile web browsers. The system consists of several major 
components, including: 
●​ User authentication and role-based profile management 
●​ Domain-restricted AI legal inquiry and legal reference retrieval 
●​ Secure legal case and proceedings workflow management 
●​ Document organization and centralized file handling 
●​ Shared legal workspaces and real-time communication tools 
●​ Automated documentation and immutable audit trail logging 
●​ Administrative monitoring, security, compliance, and user management 
The platform is intended for Filipino citizens seeking preliminary legal information, verified 
legal practitioners managing legal concerns and proceedings, and system administrators 
responsible for maintaining system operations, security, compliance, and audit monitoring. 
Overall, ALLY aims to improve accessibility, efficiency, transparency, and security in legal 
workflows while supporting secure collaboration and organized digital legal processes.

3. ​Specific Requirements  
 
3.1. ​ External interface requirements 
3.1.1.​ Hardware interfaces 
​
The platform will be designed for cross-device accessibility, ensuring compatibility 
across a wide range of hardware environments. Users will be able to access the system 
seamlessly through smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers without loss of 
functionality or performance. 
The application will be implemented as a responsive web-based system, optimized for 
different screen sizes and mobile devices to ensure usability across various smartphone 
models and operating systems. The backend infrastructure will be deployed on cloud-based 
servers to support scalability, reliability, and efficient resource management, with 
containerized deployment managed through Docker. 
For secure access across devices, the system will implement JWT-based 
authentication alongside Google OAuth integration, ensuring both security and flexibility in 
user login across supported platforms. 
3.1.2.​ Software interfaces

3.4​
Non-functional requirements 
 
Performance 
Ally shall significantly improve the speed and efficiency of accessing legal information 
compared to traditional manual processes: 
●​ The system shall return legal search results and case references within 2–3 seconds, 
reducing the need for manual legal research. 
●​ AI-generated preliminary legal guidance shall be delivered within 5 seconds, 
improving response time compared to human-only consultation. 
●​ The system shall support at least 300 concurrent users, ensuring accessibility even 
during peak usage. 
●​ Legal document uploads and retrieval shall be completed within 3–5 seconds, 
depending on file size and network conditions. 
●​ The platform shall minimize delays through optimized database queries and caching 
mechanisms.

Security 
The system shall ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and protection of sensitive legal and 
personal data: 
●​ The system shall comply with the Data Privacy Act of 2012. 
●​ All data transmissions shall be secured using HTTPS with TLS encryption. 
●​ The system shall implement role-based access control (RBAC) to restrict access to 
authorized users only. 
●​ User authentication shall use secure mechanisms such as JWT (JSON Web Tokens) 
or equivalent. 
●​ Sensitive data (e.g., personal information, legal documents) shall be encrypted at rest 
and in transit. 
●​ The system shall log user activities for monitoring suspicious behavior and auditing. 
●​ Passwords shall be securely stored using hashing algorithms (e.g., bcrypt). 
 
Reliability 
The system shall ensure consistent and dependable operation: 
●​ The system shall achieve at least 99% uptime availability, excluding scheduled 
maintenance. 
●​ Automatic data backup shall be performed regularly (e.g., daily backups). 
●​ The system shall implement error handling mechanisms to prevent crashes and 
ensure graceful failure. 
●​ In case of failure, the system shall be able to recover data with minimal loss. 
●​ Critical system components (authentication, database access) shall include fault 
tolerance mechanisms. 
●​ The system shall provide status notifications to users in case of system downtime or 
delays. 
 
Usability & Accessibility 
The system will ensure users will experience a responsive platform: 
 
●​ Cross-Platform Compatibility: 
The responsive web application must operate consistently across major modern 
browsers, including current and previous stable versions of Chrome, Firefox, and 
Microsoft Edge on desktop systems. It must also be fully functional on mobile devices 
running Android and iOS, ensuring proper rendering and usability across different 
screen sizes and operating environments.
